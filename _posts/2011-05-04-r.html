下面用R语言的lpSolve扩展包来解决一个简单的线性规划问题


target: max C = 5*x1 + 8*x2
subject to:
 x1 + x2 <= 2
 x1 + 2*x2 = 3
 x1,x2 >=0

R语言代码如下：

library(lpSolve)
eg.lp <- lp(objective.in=c(5, 8),
const.mat=matrix(c(1, 1, 1, 2), nrow=2),
const.rhs=c(2, 3),
const.dir=c("<=", "="), direction="max")
eg.lp$solution

求解为1,1
